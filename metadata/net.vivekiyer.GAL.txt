Summary:Lookup Exchange GAL for contacts
Description:
The Global Address List (GAL) lookup available in Android is limited in functionality. It only looks
up the email address and does not provide other information that is available in the GAL. This application
looks up the GAL and returns ALL data that is available on the Exchange server for the query.
