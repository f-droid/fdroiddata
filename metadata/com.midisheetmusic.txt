Summary:Graphical midi file player
Description:
MidiSheetMusic plays midi music files and highlights the notes as they play. It comes with over 50
classical piano songs, from popular composers such as Beethoven,
Mozart, and Chopin. Start learning your favourite songs on the piano today.
It's also available on the desktop platforms.

Features include:
* Adjusting the speed of the music
* Playing only the left-hand or right-hand staff
* Playing a subset of measures continuously in a loop
* Transposing the notes up or down
* Changing the instruments used for each MIDI track
.
