This is a PhoneGap port of [http://browserquest.mozilla.org BrowserQuest], the
HTML5 MMORPG (multiplayer online role-playing game) that Little Workshop
created for Mozilla. It is an old-school action-RPG in which you explore a
medieval land with your friends.

This is an early version and there are some problems with using the
onscreen keyboard to speak to others. Content is licensed as CC-BY-SA.
