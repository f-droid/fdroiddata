Auto Name:Pinboard
Summary:Bookmark manager
Description:
Pinboard is an online bookmark management service and the app itself is a port
of DeliciousDroid. Make bookmarks via the browser share menu so that they are
backed up and accessible anywhere.

Features:

* Tablet optimized
* Add, edit, view, and delete bookmarks
* Sync bookmarks and tags
* Home screen widget and live folders
* Search titles, tags, and notes.

Translations: English, Finnish, Spanish, German.
