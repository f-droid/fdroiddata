Description:
SolitaireCG is an adaptation of Ken Magic's "[[com.kmagic.solitaire]]" for
devices with few hardware buttons.

See [http://solitairecg.sourceforge.net/news.php NEWS] for changes against the
original code, and project updates.

The changes improve playability and enable card dealing spider. New hi-res
graphics have been added to the original card graphics to take advantage of
today's larger, higher resolution screens.

Solitaire Card Games include Klondike (Regular solitaire), Spider solitaire,
Freecell, Forty Thieves, and variations of these games.
