Summary:Take voice notes
Description:
Features include tagging, a high quality built-in voice recorder and the possibility to
use an external recorder or recording manager app. In addition, it can connect to an
online Estonian speech-to-text service (http://bark.phon.ioc.ee/webtrans/) to turn the recordings
into written text. The service can detect speakers in the recordings allowing for novel and powerful
ways to search, sort and playback the recordings.
