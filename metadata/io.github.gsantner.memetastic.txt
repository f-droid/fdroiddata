Summary:Simple Meme Creator
Description:
Simple Meme Creator which includes most common templates. Completly ad-free,
works offline and adds no watermarks.

Memes can be created from templates included in the app, from pictures shared
into the app and pictures from gallery & camera

There are many meme customization options available:

* Top & bottom caption
* Font & border text color
* Text size, font and capitalization

'''More Features:'''

* Show saved memes
* Create favorites
* Share into any app accepting pictures
